About the Firm

Abhishek Yadav & Co.

CS Abhishek Yadav is a Practicing Company Secretary committed to delivering reliable corporate compliance and regulatory advisory services. Experienced in handling company law matters, business registrations, and regulatory approvals for startups, businesses, and NGOs. Focused on providing practical guidance and timely compliance support to help clients operate smoothly and lawfully.

Based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, the firm provides end-to-end support to clients across UP and India — from the initial stage of business setup through to ongoing compliance management.

Areas of Specialisation

  • Corporate compliance and regulatory advisory for businesses, startups, and NGOs
  • Company law compliances and ROC filings
  • Licenses and registrations — FSSAI, Trademark, ISO, MSME, and regulatory approvals
  • Business setup, legal documentation, and ongoing compliance management
  • Practical, reliable, and timely compliance solutions for growing enterprises
